About Rowena's Salon

Rowena's Salon , also known as a hair salon or hairdressing salon, is a place where people go to get their hair styled, cut, colored, and treated. Hair salons offer a range of services related to hair care and styling, and they are typically staffed by trained hairdressers or stylists.

Our History

Rowena Hair Salon in Marinduque has a vibrant history that spans over three decades, rooted in the passion and creativity of its founder, Rodolfo Nitoral, also known as Rowena.

2010-2012

2010: Rowena embarked on her journey as a hairstylist, armed with a deep love for hairdressing and a desire to bring beauty and confidence to the people of Marinduque. With sheer determination and a commitment to excellence, she established Rowena Hair Salon, a small yet inviting space where clients could experience top-notch hair care and styling.

2012: As the salon flourished, Rowena continually honed her skills, attending workshops and training sessions to stay abreast of the latest trends and techniques in the ever-evolving world of hairstyling. Her dedication to continuous learning ensured that clients received the most up-to-date and cutting-edge services.

2015-2020

2015: Over the years, Rowena Hair Salon grew both in size and reputation, attracting a team of talented stylists who shared Rowena's passion for hairdressing. Together, they formed a close-knit family that focused on delivering exceptional results while prioritizing the comfort and satisfaction of their clients.

2020: Rowena Hair Salon continues to thrive under Rowena's guidance, embodying her values of creativity, professionalism, and genuine care for clients. With a team of skilled stylists who have been trained by Rowena herself, the salon remains at the forefront of hairstyling in Marinduque, offering a wide range of services that cater to the diverse needs and preferences of their valued clients
